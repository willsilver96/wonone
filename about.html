About SharePot

Hi, I’m Will. I’ve always loved online competitions and prize draws – from the National Lottery to little online contests – but I’ve never found one that felt truly fair or transparent. That’s why I created SharePot: an open and community-driven way to play, win, and give back.

Here’s how it works: there are four pots to choose from — 50p, £1, £2, and £5. When you enter, your money goes straight into that pot. Each pot has its own live draw and winner on Sunday at 20:00 GMT.

Every time someone enters, 20% of their entry goes directly to the selected charity linked to that pot, 5% helps keep the platform running, and the remaining 75% builds the prize pot. Each week, the charities rotate — so every pot supports a new cause.

It’s simple: pick a pot, enter, and play for a chance to win. My goal is to create a weekly buzz while sharing a bit of good along the way, where your odds of winning are better than anything else online.
