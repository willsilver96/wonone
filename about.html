About WON ONE

Hi, I’m Will. I’ve always loved online competitions and prize draws – from the National Lottery to little online contests – but I’ve never found one that felt truly fair or transparent. That’s why I created WON ONE: a simple, fun, and completely transparent way to play and win.

Here’s how it works: you pay just 11p to enter the prize draw. To qualify, you solve a question, equation, or puzzle – something small, fun, and brain-teasing. Complete it, and you’re in the draw.

For example, if 10,000 people enter at 11p each, the prize pot grows to £1,100. If everyone completes the challenge, that’s 10,000 people in the draw, giving a 1 in 10,000 chance of winning £1,100. The winner is announced every Sunday night!

For complete transparency, I don’t take any cut from the prize pot. My goal is to create a weekly buzz for just 11p, where your odds of winning are better than anything else online – no hidden tricks, no fine print, just pure fun. If you enjoy WON ONE, you’re welcome to make a small donation via PayPal to support me, and I’d be very grateful!
